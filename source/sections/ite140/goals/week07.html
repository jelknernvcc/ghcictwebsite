Overview

This week we will be introduced to techniques for visualization of our data using Matplotlib.

Goals for Week 7

This week we will:

  • complete the following values survey. We will use the results as a data source for the whole process of sanitization, cleansing, transforming, and finally visualizing data.
  • read the Plotting section of Chapter 5 - Data Analysis with pandas from our text, trying out all the examples. NOTE: Your git commits for this will be your first grade for week 7.
  • apply the plotting techniques learned to produce visualizations of our own data sets. NOTE: Your presentation of this in class on Friday will be your second grade for week 7.
