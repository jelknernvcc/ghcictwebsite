Overview

With our weekend task completed, we will continue reading Chapter 5: Data Analysis with pandas with an eye towards understanding what the pandas library contains and how to use the DataFrame objects within it.

Goals for Week 4

This week we will:

  • start class on Tuesday by sharing presentations on the vocabulary from the first two paragraphs of chapter 5. We will hear about Jupyter notebooks from Aaron, pandas from Christopher, interfaces from Julian, data sets from Marin, statistics from Ahmad, vectorization and data alignment from Jamethiel, visualization from Kaleb, cleaning data from Jeff, and time series, aggregation and descriptive statistics from Antonio.
  • learn to use pandas to load DataFrames from xlsx files and to access the data within them.
  • begin to explore open datasets, including where to find them and how to access their data.
