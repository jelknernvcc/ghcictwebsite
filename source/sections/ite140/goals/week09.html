Week of Tuesday, October 25, 2022

Overview

This week we will set up our development environments for PostgreSQL, and begin A Curious Moon.

Goals for Week 9

We meet twice this week (Tuesday and Thursday), during which times we will:

  1. present the case study code from Chapter 7: Excel File Manipulation with pandas.
  2. install PostgreSQL on our laptops.
  3. distribute A Curious Moon.
  4. read the first chapter, Lift Off, which introduces our protagonist, Dee, and sets the stage for her adventure.
  5. select a pair to present a summary of the chapter to the class.

Ahmad and Antonio were chosen to present a summary of the first chapter, Lift Off, of A Curious Moon.

Everyone is responsible for reading the chapter, and a short quiz at the beginning of class on Thursday with questions based on the reading is fair game.
