ITE 140: Week 1

Week of Monday, August 26, 2024

Overview

Welcome to Database Design and Management! This is the first year we are offering this course, so you will you be in some ways pioneers in uncharted territory. The big goal will be to learn a collection of useful skills and explore ideas related to data science.

During the Fall semester this year long dual-enrolled course will be dual-enrolled with ITE 140: Spreadsheet Software. If you took that course on campus at NVCC, you would find it a course about how to use Microsoft Excel.

What we will do here will be very different. Excel won't even be required, since we will be using Python to process data stored in xlsx files. The book we will use for the class is Python for Excel: A Modern Environment for Automation and Data Analysis, which you can access through the O'Reilly for Higher Education account that NVCC provides you.

Goals for Week 1

Our main tasks for this week will be to gain access to to the educational resources we will need, setup our development environments, and to begin learning the Python we will need for the course.
