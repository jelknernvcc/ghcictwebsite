ITN 170: Linux System Administration

Assignments

We will be using Red Hat RHCSA/RHCE 7 Cert Guide: Red Hat Enterprise Linux 7 (EX200 and EX300) as our text, since it is available on-line through NVCC's Safari Books Online subscription.

We have 23 weeks to learn the material in 24 chapters of an intensive curriculum. Since we will want the last 3 weeks to review and prepare for the exam, that determines a pace of 6/5 chapters per week. We also need to keep in mind the usual disruptions that occur for SOL testing, AP exams, etc. The botton line is we will need to stay focused, stay on task, and work hard if we are going to be ready to take the RHCSA exam by June.
