Overview

This week we will run our first programs on the MITS Altair Simulator, learning to program it with machine code.

Wednesday, September 4th

Today we will work through Part 3: Operation of the Altair 8800 together, entering and running our first program. Before we begin, download zero_ram.bin.

Process

We are going to perform a modified version of the adding two numbers program presented in Part 3: Operation of the Altair 8800 - B: Loading a Sample Program. Our modification will be to replace the JMP instruction at step 11 with a HLT instruction. The version in the manual uses the JMP to put the program into an infinite loop.
