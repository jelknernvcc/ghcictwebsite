Jelkner's Presentation Grading Rubric

Grade Description
A Information is complete and correct, and presents information beyond what was directly given in the assigned readings or task (in other words, evidence of individual investitation). Presentation is clear and engaging, demonstrating mastery of the learning objectives. The work appears to have been developed by people who take pride in their work.
B Information is mostly complete and correct, and fully presents all the relevant information given in the assigned readings or task. Presentation is clear and engaging, and demonstrates mastery of the learning objectives. The work shows evidence of competence and looks like it was completed by people willing to put in the time needed to get the job done.
C Information is partially complete and mostly correct, but important elements are missing or inaccurate.
D A presentation was made and perhaps presented, but is shows only minimal effort and does not provide sufficient evidence of learning mastery.
E No presentation was prepared and presented.
